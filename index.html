Detailed Stream Distribution (Std. 11th & 12th)

Our institution successfully operates 4 major specialized learning streams designed to meet diverse student career goals:

1. Faculty of ARTS

A comprehensive track focusing on civil services, humanities, and languages.

  • Core Compulsory: English & Marathi / Hindi
  • Political Science & History
  • Economics & Sociology
  • Environmental Science

2. Faculty of SCIENCE

High-tech standard learning division for core engineering, medical, and technological domains.

  • General Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics (60 Seats)
  • Information Technology (IT): Modern alternative elective track (60 Seats)
  • Computer Science: Bifurcated technical vocational engine (50 Seats)
  • Electronics: Hardware system and peripherals management (50 Seats)
  • Crop Science: Organic Grower & Irrigation techniques (50 Seats)

3. Faculty of COMMERCE (English Medium)

Tailored explicitly for business administration, corporate management, chartered accounts, and banking loops.

  • Book Keeping & Accountancy
  • Organization of Commerce (OC)
  • Secretarial Practice (SP) & Economics
  • English, Marathi, & Environmental Studies

4. Faculty of MCVC / HSCVC (Vocational)

Direct standalone professional employment modules guaranteeing financial self-reliance.

  • Automobile Technology: Engine mechanics, body servicing, & wiring diagnostics. Renders direct entry right into 2nd year Polytechnic engineering!
  • Crop Science (Vocational): Advanced farming, agro-forestry, and seed management. Direct eligibility for state Gramsevak and Krisevak public posts.
